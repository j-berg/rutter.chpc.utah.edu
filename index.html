Integrating multi- or single-omic metabolic data upon the metabolic network can be challenging for a variety of reasons. Metaboverse seeks to simplify this task for users by providing a simple, user-friendly interface for layering their data on a dynamic representation of the metabolic network and automatically searching the network for interesting regulatory or other patterns.
